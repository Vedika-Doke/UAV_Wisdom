# Frames, Motors & Propellers

## Frames
| Class | Prop size | Typical use |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Tinywhoop | 31–40 mm | Indoor |
| Cinewhoop | 2.5–3" | Indoor/outdoor cinematic |
| Freestyle | 5" | General FPV |
| Long-range | 7–10" | Cruise / mapping |
| Heavy-lift | 10"+ | Cinema payloads |

## Motors
> *Cover: KV rating, stator size (e.g. 2207, 2306), thrust-to-weight target (>4:1 for freestyle), efficiency vs power.*

## Propellers
> *Cover: diameter × pitch × blade count, bi-blade vs tri-blade tradeoffs, material (polycarbonate vs reinforced).*
