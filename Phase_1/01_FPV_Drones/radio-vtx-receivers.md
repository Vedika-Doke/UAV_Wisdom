# Radio Link, VTX & Receivers

## RC link options
| Tech | Band | Range | Latency | Notes |
|------|------|-------|---------|-------|
| ExpressLRS (ELRS) | 2.4 GHz / 900 MHz | Very long | Very low | Open source, current default |
| TBS Crossfire | 900 MHz | Long | Low | Mature, commercial |
| FrSky ACCESS / D16 | 2.4 GHz | Medium | Low | Legacy hobbyist |
| DJI O3 / O4 | 2.4 / 5.8 GHz | Long | Low | Closed, digital video bundled |
