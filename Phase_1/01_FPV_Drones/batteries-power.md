# Batteries & Power Systems

## LiPo basics
- Cell count (S): 1S ≈ 3.7 V nominal, 4.2 V full. Common: 4S, 6S.
- Capacity (mAh) — flight time.
- C-rating — peak current = C × (capacity / 1000) A.
- Li-Ion 18650/21700 packs for long-range cruise (high energy density, lower C).

## Power distribution
> *PDB, AIO power module, voltage regulators (5 V / 3.3 V / 9 V for VTX), current sensing for telemetry.*
