# PX4

Modular autopilot from the Pixhawk / Dronecode team. Apache-2.0 licensed → friendly to commercial use.

## Strengths
- Apache-2.0 license (vs ArduPilot's GPL)
- Modular architecture (uORB pub/sub between modules)
- NuttX RTOS underneath
- Strong commercial backing (Auterion, NXP, Skydio history)

## Limitations
- Smaller community than ArduPilot
- Fewer vehicle types out of the box

## Architecture (notes)
- NuttX RTOS
- uORB inter-module messaging
- ECL/EKF2 estimator
