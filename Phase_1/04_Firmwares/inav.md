# INAV

Fork of Betaflight/Cleanflight focused on **navigation** — waypoints, return-to-home, fixed-wing support.

## Strengths
- Fixed-wing and multirotor in one firmware
- Simpler to set up than ArduPilot for hobby autonomy
- Uses same configurator paradigm as Betaflight

## Limitations
- Less mature than ArduPilot/PX4 for commercial autonomy
- Smaller dev community
