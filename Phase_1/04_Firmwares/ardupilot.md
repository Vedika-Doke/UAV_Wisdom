# ArduPilot

The most mature open-source autopilot. Origin: APM project (2010s) → ArduPilot.

## Vehicles supported
Copter · Plane · Rover · Sub · Blimp · AntennaTracker · Heli

## Strengths
- Widest vehicle support, very mature autonomy
- ChibiOS RTOS, deterministic
- Huge documentation, active forum
- Mission Planner / QGC GCS support

## Limitations
- GPL-licensed → can complicate closed-source commercial use
- Steeper learning curve than Betaflight

## Architecture (notes)
- AP_HAL hardware abstraction → portable across boards
- EKF3 attitude/position estimator
